Community Involvement

HIV SEER's Program

I am a member and Peer Educator of the SEERs (Stigma reduction through Education, Empowerment and Research) program, a global community-based participatory research project designed to reduce HIV stigma among youth, which is a known barrier to HIV testing and treatment.
Since its founding in 2016 by Dr. Tiffany Chenneville of the University of South Florida, our teams have delivered the program, collectively, to over 3,000 youth in local schools and churches in the city of Nakuru, Kenya.

Heroes For Change Program

I'm also a member of Heroes For Change program which is a volunteer programme by Uniliver Kenya that is designed to enable young Kenyans from around the country to help deliver behavior change programmes, educating children, new moms and their new families' handwashing with soap, boosting their iron uptake and brushing teeth day and night.
